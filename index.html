I'm Juan Luis Ovalle, a 17-year-old teenager passionate about technology and artificial intelligence.

I run Yeilow Tech, a teaching channel about technology, finance, and the latest innovations in artificial intelligence, which right now is on pause due to my busyness level this year in particular.

At Monte Tabor School, I took various subjects, from programming and statistics to calculus and physics. Simultaneously, I pursued additional courses in finance, 3D design using Unreal Engine, and mainly programming, particularly through Harvard University's CS50 in Computer Science and CS50 in Artificial Intelligence. These courses together provided over 400 hours of comprehensive knowledge, significantly enhancing my understanding of abstraction and design in computing.

I am currently developing an application designed to visualize financial topics. This app allows users to monitor their investment portfolios, including stocks, cryptocurrencies, and bonds from around the world. My goal is to present this information in a user-friendly and visually appealing manner, making it easier for people to understand and manage their investments.
